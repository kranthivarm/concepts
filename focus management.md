## Question 1: What is Deep Work?

- Deep work means concentrating deeply on a task without distractions.  
- Social media and unnecessary things should be avoided to achieve this.  
- It enhances the quality of learning and outcomes.

---

## Question 2: According to the author of the Deep Work book, how to do deep work properly?

- We should avoid using mobile phones or other gadgets unnecessarily during deep work.  
- We should schedule deep work sessions properly.  
- We should use only productive tools.

---

## Question 3: How can you implement the principles in your day-to-day life?

- We should log distractions so that they can be avoided next time.  
- We should schedule deep work sessions properly.  
- Follow the same timings and record unfinished tasks so they can be resolved next time.  
- Start slowly with small sessions like 1 hour, and try to increase them day by day.

---

## Question 4: What are the dangers of social media?

- A lot of time is wasted.  
- It makes it hard to focus.  
- Frequent use can make people lazy and lonely.  
- It distracts us and breaks concentration.
