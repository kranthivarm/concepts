# Prevention of Sexual Harassment (PoSH)

## What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

- Unnecessary or unwanted physical contact  
- Repeated personal or sexual remarks, jokes, or invitations  
- Staring or gestures of a sexual nature  

## What should you do if you face or witness such behaviour?

- If safe, tell the person clearly that their behaviour is unwelcome and must stop  
- Document the incident  
- Report to your Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) or HR  
- Maintain confidentiality and respect the dignity of all parties involved
