## Question 1: Which point(s) were new to you?

- The importance of **over-communication** in software development.  
- Joining meetings **5–10 minutes early** to build rapport with teammates and improve collaboration.  
- The concept of **deep work** and actively protecting focus by reducing distractions such as social media notifications during work hours.

---

## Question 2: Which area do you think you need to improve on? What are your ideas to make progress in that area?

### Areas of Improvement

- Clearly explaining problems instead of asking vague questions.  
- Communicating progress or delays proactively.  
- Maintaining consistent focus during work sessions.  

### Ideas to Make Progress

- Use screenshots or small code snippets to explain issues clearly.  
- Share updates regularly with the team instead of waiting until deadlines.  
- Follow deep work principles.  
- Track daily productivity using a time-tracking app.
